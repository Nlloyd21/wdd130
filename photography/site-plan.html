Overview

Purpose

As a photographer, my goal is to provide easy access for those who are looking for a photographer to take/edit photos for them, whether it be for school photos, wedding, etc. On the website, before a potential customer wants to book a photoshoot, they can view information about me and my background as a professional photographer, as well as view some example photographs that I have previously taken/edited for other customers, as well as some of my favorite photographs that I have taken for my own pleasure, in which it will help them make their decision of booking a photoshoot.

Audience

This website is aimed at anyone that likes photograpahy and want to view some of my work, and/or are on the website to book a potential photoshoot that they might want for an event, such as a birthday, wedding, or just something simple as senior photos.

Content

Home page

Hello and thank you for visiting my portfolio and seeing some of my favorite works that I have completed. Here is a little about myself. I grew up mainly in Illinois for 17 years of my life. My senior year of high school, we moved to New Hampshire; one of the prettiest states I have ever seen. I have always loved photograpahy ever since I was little, but I have always wanted to learn more. So I decided to take a photography classmy senior year of high school. I was able to do some amazing projects in that class, and really experiment with photoshop, photography styles, colors, etc. I am now studying at Brigham Young University-Idaho and have been continuing photography in my freetime.
